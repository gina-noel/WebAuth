Audience, Issues and Approach (AIA) by Gina Noel

Client's name and decision maker Steven Ross
Business name 8 Element Solutions (8ElementSolutions.com)
Redesign or new website Brand new website
Type of website Interactive personal coaching using an 8 part wheel solution
Client's goals Interactive personal coaching content to help clients create their own wheel through an online content course that helps people help themselves become more balanced in life as well as an option for more personalized coaching.

Audiences Issues, Concerns, Needs Approach
Older individuals between the ages 40-65 who are looking to improve different areas of their life. These individuals will most likely be more focused on finances, health, fun, community and Lifestyle. These individuals will be competent with navigating a website and downloading and purchasing purchased materials for home use.
  • Keeping clients motivated
  • Clients asking for qualifications
  • Inspiring new clientele
  • Returns
  • Addressing content for an older population that may not want to focus on 8 areas
  • Content maintenance
  • Develop a weekly/biweekly training session video - make this viewable until the next training session
  • Create an About section
  • SEO - drive search results to website
  • Create a content workbook that can be purchased
  • Shipping - find an online vendor to handle orders
  • Return policy needs to be created - maybe a 30-day money back guarantee
  • Monitor website for updates or new offerings
Active younger individuals between the ages of 18-40 who are into yoga, running, cycling, hiking or any self improvement. These younger individuals will most likely be women who are overwhelmed with balancing family, career challenges, Learning, adaption and trying to do it all while still having fun. The younger generation will have zero trouble navigating, downloading or purchasing a web site from either a tablet, phone or personal computer.
  • Looking for more community
  • Having content ideas that is focused on a younger generation
  • Looking for a faster paced program
  • Pricing
  • Online content that does not need to be printed
  • Create Facebook, Instagram and Twitter accounts
  • Research what areas younger clients are more focused on
  • Offer different levels of personalization
  • Determine what price point will draw in the younger generation
  • Create a shopping cart
  • Create material that can be filled in from either a computer, tablet or cell phone
Personal 8 element coaching for a more one on one experience for individuals who need more of a personal touch. These users will be a mix of the above two sections and will need to access materials by a tablet, cell phone or computer as well as have the ability to join a zoom call for weekly or biweekly personal meetings
  • Technical difficulties
  • Booking or scheduling system
  • Contact information
  • Email detailed instructions on computer setup
  • Create contact form
  • Create a shopping cart with different offerings
Services within the self help industry; for example, yoga retreats, workshops, new age book stores and other vendors in the self-help or spiritual industry. The focus for the 8 elements will be: Financial, Health, Adaption, Community, Spirituality, Learning, Fun, and Lifestyle. Materials will need to be printed. My client is still considering this as an option and probably needs more thought into just this one topic.
  • Not able to connect users with other services
  • Services being offered may not fit into a faster paced session
  • The 8 element wheel will need to not be as personalized
  • Pricing for larger groups
  • Training
  • Materials
  • Offer a few courses to friends for feedback and experience
  • Create a detailed 8 element wheel with less customer input
  • Research what other similar services are charging - be a customer
  • Consider training someone who specializes in training groups
  • Price out material costs and add into the pricing
